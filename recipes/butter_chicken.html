3. Butter Chicken


Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a popular Indian dish made with tender chicken pieces cooked in a rich and creamy tomato-based sauce. It is known for its buttery flavor and mildly spiced gravy, making it a favorite among many.

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala, turmeric powder, and red chili powder. Add the chicken pieces and marinate for at least 1 hour or overnight in the refrigerator.
  2. Heat butter in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Sauté for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the tomato puree and cook for 10-15 minutes until the oil starts to separate from the mixture.
  5. Add the marinated chicken pieces to the pan. Cook until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.
  6. Stir in the heavy cream and fenugreek leaves. Simmer for another 5-10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  7. Add salt to taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
